Mental Health Outreach · SDG 3心理健康推廣・永續發展目標3
"Mental health is not a luxury — it is a prerequisite for learning, teaching, and human flourishing." 「心理健康不是奢侈品——它是學習、教學和人類繁盛的前提。」

Aligned with the UN's Sustainable Development Goal 3 (Good Health & Well-Being), this work bridges educational research with community mental health advocacy — across Taiwan, Africa, and beyond. 與聯合國永續發展目標3（良好健康與福祉）對齊，這項工作將教育研究與社區心理健康倡議相連結——跨越台灣、非洲及其他地區。

Core Research Themes核心研究主題

What I research 研究方向

01
AI & Digital LiteracyAI 與數位素養

Exploring how AI tools reshape learning environments and what digital literacy means for 21st-century educators and students.探索AI工具如何重塑學習環境，以及數位素養對21世紀教育者和學生的意義。

02
Social Emotional Learning社會情感學習

Developing SEL frameworks that integrate emotional intelligence, resilience, and well-being across diverse classroom and cultural contexts.開發整合情感智慧、韌性和福祉的SEL框架，適用於多元課堂和文化背景。

03
Mental Health & SDG 3心理健康與永續發展目標3

Advocating for mental health integration in education aligned with the UN SDG 3 (Good Health & Well-Being) framework.倡導將心理健康整合到教育中，與聯合國永續發展目標3（良好健康與福祉）框架對齊。

04
Cross-Cultural Education跨文化教育

Building pedagogical bridges between Taiwan, Africa, and Asia through exchange programs, intercultural research, and collaborative curricula.透過交流計劃、跨文化研究和協作課程，在台灣、非洲和亞洲之間建立教育橋樑。

05
Mandarin as Second Language中文作為第二語言

Designing Mandarin acquisition programs for international students, with tailored approaches for African and Southeast Asian learners.為國際學生設計中文習得計劃，針對非洲和東南亞學習者制定客製化方法。

06
Technology-Enhanced Pedagogy科技增強教學法

Investigating how technology can amplify teacher effectiveness and student engagement across STEM and humanities disciplines.研究科技如何在STEM和人文學科中增強教師效能和學生參與度。
